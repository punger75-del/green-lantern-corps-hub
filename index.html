Eras of the Emerald Knight

The Golden Age: Alan Scott (1940)

Lore Unlike the intergalactic Corps, Alan Scott's power was mystical. He forged a ring and lantern from the Starheart, an ancient meteor containing the Green Flame of Life. His primary weakness was Wood, a limitation born from the Starheart's magical nature.

The Silver Age: Hal Jordan (1959)

Publication DC rebooted the concept as hard Sci-Fi. Starpilot Abin Sur crashed on Earth and commanded his ring to find a successor "utterly honest and born without fear." It chose test pilot Hal Jordan.

The Emerald Twilight & Rebirth

Major Event Following the destruction of Coast City, Hal Jordan suffered a psychotic break, destroyed the Corps, and became the villain Parallax. This was later retconned in GL: Rebirth to reveal that Parallax was a sentient fear entity that had possessed Jordan through the ring's Yellow Impurity.

Lantern Personnel Files

Keli Quintela (Teen Lantern)

Sector 2814 An 11-year-old genius from La Paz, Bolivia. After witnessing a Green Lantern (Iorl) being murdered in a junkyard, she researched the Corps on her sister's phone and "hacked" the Lantern technology into a powerful alien gauntlet. She is considered a wildcard in the Corps hierarchy.

Tomar-Re (Sector 2813)

Honor Guard A Xudarian starpilot and the first alien to mentor Hal Jordan. He famously carried the guilt of failing to save the planet Krypton—he had been en route with stellar-suppressor uranium when a solar flare blinded him, causing him to arrive just as the planet exploded.

Mogo (Sector 2261)

Living Planet A sentient planet that is also a member of the Corps. Mogo is the entity responsible for guiding every new Power Ring to its destined recruit across the 3600 sectors of the universe.
